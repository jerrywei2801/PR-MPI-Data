PR-FFL-MPI
Dataset of multi-angle FFL system.

All experiments were conducted under a selection-field gradient of 4 T/m/ μ0 busing a dual-frequency excitation scheme, consisting of a high-frequency drive field with an amplitude of 30 mT/μ0 at 10 kHz and a low-frequency component of 5 mT/μ0 at 500 Hz. 2D imaging was carried out with spatial sampling ranges of -9 mm to 9 mm along both directions and a step size of 1 mm, resulting in a system matrix of size 19 
× 19. The sampling rate was set to 2.5 MS/s, and the signal acquisition time for each measurement was 1 s.
